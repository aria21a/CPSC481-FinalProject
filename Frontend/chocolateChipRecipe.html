Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe Steps

  1. 1. Make the chocolate chip cookie dough:

    • Combine dry ingredients in a medium bowl.
    • Measure flour correctly using a measuring cup for dry ingredients and leveling with a knife.
    • Cream together softened butter and sugars.
    • Add eggs and vanilla, then beat to combine.
    • Add dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
    • Add chocolate chips and beat until evenly distributed.

  2. 2. Portion and roll dough:

    • Use a cookie scoop to ensure uniform size for even baking.

  3. 3. Bake the cookies:

    • For fresh dough: Roll dough into balls and place on a baking sheet.
    • Bake at 375°F for 8-10 minutes, do not over-bake.
    • Cookies will be slightly doughy when removed from the oven, but will set as they cool.

  4. 4. Cool and store:

    • Thaw first: Thaw on a baking sheet at room temperature for 1-2 hours, then bake at 375°F as directed.
    • Bake from frozen: Preheat oven to 350°F, place frozen dough on a sheet, and bake for 15 minutes.
    • Cool cookies on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
    • Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days or freeze for up to 2 months.
